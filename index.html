Workshop on Emerging Challenges and Innovations in ICT in the Era of AI

UCC: Unified Complex Computing

Join us for a transformative workshop that delves into the forefront of Information and Communication Technology (ICT) and Computer Science. Organized by the University of Messina and the University of Catania, this event is designed to foster collaboration among experts, researchers, and professionals who are navigating the evolving landscape of ICT in the age of artificial intelligence.

Supported by the FF4ALL Project

This workshop is supported by the FF4ALL project that addresses the issues of disinformation, infodemic, and propaganda, which contribute to Cognitive Warfare—efforts to influence public perception and trust in media and institutions. The initiative, coordinated by UNISA, integrates intelligence analysis, artificial intelligence, political studies, data science, and web intelligence to develop tools and methodologies for counteracting information disorder. It includes several research areas, such as deepfake detection, decision support for cyber intelligence, and media authentication. The project fosters a multidisciplinary approach to monitor, detect, and mitigate the spread of misinformation, promoting trust and critical awareness in digital information ecosystems.

Why Attend?

This workshop presents a unique opportunity to engage with thought leaders and innovators in the field. Participants will gain insights into the latest trends, challenges, and opportunities that are shaping the future of digital transformation. Whether you are looking to expand your knowledge, network with peers, or share your research, this event is tailored for you.

Workshop Highlights

The workshop will cover a range of groundbreaking topics, including but not limited to:

  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning: Explore advancements in AI technologies that are revolutionizing industries.
  • Generative Models for Visual Tasks: Understand how generative models are enhancing visual data processing and creativity.
  • Big Data and Cloud Computing: Discuss strategies for managing and analyzing vast amounts of data in cloud environments.
  • Cybersecurity and Privacy: Address the critical issues surrounding data protection and cybersecurity measures.
  • Internet of Things (IoT) and Smart Systems: Learn about the integration of IoT technologies in creating smarter environments.
  • Edge Computing vs. High-Performance Computing: Compare these two paradigms and their applications in modern computing.
  • Deepfake Detection and Retrieval Methods: Investigate techniques for identifying deepfakes and ensuring content authenticity, featuring insights from the FF4ALL project.

  • Industry Professionals looking to stay updated on technological advancements.
